## How to find and buy ~drugs~ a car, Backstage

1. From the main menu -- when you start the game, or travel to a Festival Site - in the "Buy & Sell" tab, open "Horizon Backstage"
   ![Buy Backstage](../images/menu-buy-backstage.png)
   If you're out driving around, you can find it in the "Cars" tab of the menu.
   ![Buy Backstage](../images/menu-cars-backstage.png)
1. Choose the "Backstage Shop"  
   If you have 0 Backstage Passes, see the next section for how to get one.
1. Press the right button to Sort by "Manufacturer"  
   Now you can "Jump to Manufacturer", and all that normal stuff
1. Once you've found the car you can buy in with your Backstage Pass

## How to buy a Backstage Pass with normal, boring Credits

From the main menu, when you start the game, in the "Buy & Sell" tab, open "Forzathon! Shop".
   ![Shop](../images/menu-buy-forzathon.png)

If you're out driving around, you'll need to open the "Cars" menu, "Buy New & Used Cars", then travel to the Festival Site. That will get you back to the main menu. Then open "Forzathon! Shop".
   ![Shop](../images/menu-buy-forzathon-shop.png)

Purchase a 1X Pass, if one is available.

Your FP (Forza Points) are shown at the bottom.

You can buy one pass, then you have to wait for the Time Remaining to buy another.
